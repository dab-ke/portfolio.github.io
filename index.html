Hello, my name is Dabraham Immorah, also known as Dab. I'm a creative artist based in Nairobi, Kenya with a great passion for commercial modeling and photography. Back in 2018 when I had attended a fashion show event, I gained interest and that's how I got to learn about commercial modeling. I am still new to the industry but very eager to learn and grow as a creative. With my little experience I am excited to collaborate with different creatives and models to see where this journey takes me. I am looking to partnering with huge companies in the near future.

Featured Work

I collaborated with the amazing Risper (@pu.m.k.in) on a stunning piece of art. We drew inspiration from the incredible seth_gor on Instagram and came up with a reel.
The reel managed to get over 7,000 plays, 600 likes, and around 40 comments. It is posted on our instagram accounts so click on the picture to give it a look.

It was an incredible honor to collaborate with Mutua Matheka (@truthslinger) on his Nywele series where he beautifully captures the essence of African Hair. As one of my favorite photographers, being his hair model for this project truly feels like a dream come true. The experience was not only inspiring but also empowering.
